Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a flatmate so we can split rent?
Yes! You just need to make your request on the platform by clicking the button above; we will be contacting you afterwards
What if I have a space and I need a tenant/flatmate?
No worries. Once we receive your request and we are able to successfully make our verification, you will be connected to people who meet your criteria and vice versa
Do I pay to get verified?
No you don't! You are only charged a fee when the pairing is done, and a base fee when you pay for your chosen apartment. You won't be breaking the bank
How does it work?
You need a place close to work, or just a crib to lay your head? We're here for you. We connect you to verified home owners and potential flatmates. This means you only pay a fraction of the entire rent while your flatmate(s) pay the rest. it's far better than it sounds, trust us!

About Us

BQ is an end-to-end platform that connects potential verified users based on their preferred matching criteria (flatmate match). We connect matched flatmates to houses that suit their needs (house match). Flatmates can then choose to split the rent which will be paid through the BQ platform..
